How to Play 游戏指南

Objective: Park your car in the designated spot without hitting obstacles 目标：将车停在指定位置，避免碰撞障碍物

Tips: Take your time and plan your approach carefully 提示：保持耐心，仔细规划停车路线

About My Parking Lot 关于我的停车场

My Parking Lot is an engaging parking simulation game that challenges your driving and spatial awareness skills. With realistic physics and various challenging scenarios, you'll need to master precise control and careful planning to become a parking expert. 我的停车场是一款引人入胜的停车模拟游戏，挑战你的驾驶技术和空间感知能力。通过真实的物理效果和各种具有挑战性的场景，你需要掌握精确的控制和谨慎的规划才能成为停车高手。
