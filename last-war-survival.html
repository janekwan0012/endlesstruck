How to Play

游戏指南

Controls

控制方式

• Mouse: Select units and buildings
• Left Click: Move units, attack enemies
• Right Click: Cancel actions
• Keyboard: Camera movement (WASD)

• 鼠标：选择单位和建筑
• 左键点击：移动单位，攻击敌人
• 右键点击：取消操作
• 键盘：相机移动（WASD）

Objectives

游戏目标

1. Build and defend your base
2. Gather resources and upgrade facilities
3. Train and command your army
4. Defeat enemy forces and survive

1. 建造和保卫你的基地
2. 收集资源并升级设施
3. 训练和指挥你的军队
4. 击败敌军并生存下来

Tips

游戏技巧

1. Balance resource gathering and defense
2. Upgrade key buildings first
3. Use terrain advantages in battles
4. Keep a reserve of resources for emergencies

1. 平衡资源收集和防御建设
